About Lord Venkateswara

Lord Venkateswara, also known as Balaji, Srinivasa, or Govinda, is an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. The Tirupati temple located in Tirumala Hills is one of the richest and most visited temples in the world.

Devotees believe praying here removes sins and grants prosperity and peace.
